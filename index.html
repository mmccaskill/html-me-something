Places I've Lived

  • Sedalia, MO
  • Clinton, MO
  • Overland Park, KS
  • Lawrence, KS
  • Kansas City, MO

Sedalia, MO

"I was born in Sediala, MO, the home of the Missouri state fair. I only have a few memories of the place as I move slightly prior to my fourth birthday. My Dad traveled with his job and we had to move a few times when I was little. Of the memories I have, one is going to the fair and getting cotton candy, another is going to the park climbing on a historic locomotive and the other is playing with the neighbors black dog."

Clinton, MO

"Some of my favorite childhood memories were living in Clinton, MO. Following my Dad on the the golf course. Seeing movies with my sister at the theater of the town square, learning how to swim and going to my first high school football and basketball games."

Overland Park, KS

"When I was 8 we moved to Overland Park and remained in the same home until I went to college. Went to Shawnee Mission South High School where I played football."

Lawrence, KS

"I moved to Lawrence as I pursued a business degree at The University of Kansas."

Kansas City, MO

"After college I moved to KC. There I lived on the Plaza and in Brookside. I love the feel of the area."
